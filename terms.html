We take intellectual property seriously. If you are a copyright holder and find content that belongs to you or your organization, you may submit a Notice of Infringement.

Please contact the administrator via the official support channels with: 1. Identification of the copyrighted work, 2. The URL on our site, and 3. Proof of ownership. Valid requests are typically processed within 48 to 72 business hours.

Unauthorized data mining is strictly prohibited. To maintain the integrity of our hosting environment and protect our proprietary summaries:

Users shall not attempt to inject malicious code, disrupt the site’s performance, or redistribute the content of this Blog on commercial platforms or for-profit academic portals.

Permitted Use:

Printing for personal study, referencing in moot court memos (with proper citation), and sharing links for peer-to-peer non-commercial learning is encouraged.
