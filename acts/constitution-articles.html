Constitution of India

448 Articles • 25 Parts • 12 Schedules

The Structure

Originally 395 Articles, now expanded to ~448 via various amendments, organized into 25 Parts.

12 Schedules

Contains categorized lists like States, Salaries, Oaths, and Allocation of seats in Rajya Sabha.

Amendments

Article 368 gives Parliament the power to amend, keeping the 'Basic Structure' intact.
