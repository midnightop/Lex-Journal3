Protocols & Mission.

Calcutta Law Journal ka uddeshya legal scholars aur students ko ek aisi platform dena hai jahan unki research global standards ko meet kare. Hum strictly peer-reviewed content ko promote karte hain jo modern jurisprudence ko challenge kare.

01. Intellectual Integrity

AI Limit: 25%

Har submission original hona chahiye. AI tools (ChatGPT/Gemini) ka upyog sirf structure refine karne ke liye 25% tak allow hai. Baaki 75% purely human research honi chahiye.

02. Technical Compliance

Format: PDF (A4)

Citations aur Bluebook formatting ko maintain karne ke liye hum sirf PDF files accept karte hain. Word files ya screenshots turant reject ho jayenge.

03. Niche Authority

Scope: Strictly Legal

Hum sirf Corporate Law, Constitutional Law, Legal Maxims, aur Landmark Case Analysis par research papers accept karte hain. General topics allow nahi hain.

04. Commercial Consent

Monetization: Enabled

Submission ka matlab hai ki aap humein permission dete hain ki hum platform maintenance ke liye aapke content par ads aur promotional material run kar sakein.
