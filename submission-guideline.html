Submission Terms &
Editorial Guidelines

01. AI Threshold

Manuscripts must not exceed 25% AI assistance. We prioritize original human jurisprudence and analytical reasoning.

02. PDF Format

To maintain Bluebook citation integrity, submissions are accepted in PDF format only. Other extensions will be ignored.

03. Niche Scope

Submissions must focus on Case Law, Legal Maxims, or Constitutional Policy. Generic content falls outside our scope.

04. Promo Rights

Authors grant us the right to run advertisements on published works to sustain our open-access hosting fees.

05. Certificate

All published authors will be awarded a Certificate of Publication, recognizing their contribution as legal researchers.
