Submission Terms & Conditions

1. AI Usage & Integrity

Academic originality is paramount. Submissions must not exceed 25% AI-generated content (ChatGPT/Gemini). Exceeding this limit leads to immediate disqualification.

2. Document Format

To maintain citation and document integrity, we accept PDF format only. Other formats like .docx or .txt will not be processed.

3. Subject Niche

We exclusively accept Legal Research Papers, Case Commentaries, and Legal Maxims. Non-legal content is outside our editorial scope.

4. Advertisement Rights

The Journal reserves the right to use submitted content for promotional and marketing purposes across our digital platforms.

5. Monetization

To sustain platform hosting and maintenance, third-party advertisements may be displayed alongside or within the published content.
