Naye Labour Laws 2026: Aapki Salary aur Office Life mein kya badlega?

Anusmita Das

Durgapur institute of legal study | kazi Nazrul University

Labour Laws ka naam sunte hi aksar humein lagta hai ki ye sirf badi companies ke liye hain. Lekin 2026 ke naye rules **aapke pocket aur aapke parivaar** par seedha asar dalte hain. Chaliye samajhte hain ki naye 4 Labour Codes aapki zindagi kaise badalne wale hain.

1. Salary ki "Nayi Definition": Khushi ya Chinta?

Ab tak companies salary ko kai hisson (allowances) mein baant deti thi. Lekin ab:

The 50% Rule

Aapki "Basic Salary" total CTC ka kam se kam 50% honi chahiye. Iska matlab hai allowances ab 50% se zyada nahi ho sakte.

PF Mein Fayda

Kyunki Basic badhegi, toh aapka PF contribution bhi badhega. Retirement ke waqt aapke paas pehle se kahin zyada bada fund hoga.

In-Hand Salary ka Sach

Sawal ye hai ki kya hath mein aane wale paise kam ho jayenge? Haan, shayad thode kam ho jayein kyunki PF mein zyada paisa katega, lekin ye aapki future savings ke liye behtareen hai.

2. 4-Day Work Week: Sach ya Sapna?

Naye kanoon mein working hours ko lekar kafi flexibility di gayi hai. Lekin iski ek shart hai:

Agar aap 4 din kaam karna chahte hain:

  • Aapko din mein 12 ghante kaam karna hoga.
  • Hafte mein total working hours 48 hours se zyada nahi ho sakte.
  • Isse aapko hafte mein 3 din ki chutti mil sakti hai.

*Ye company aur employee ki aapsi sehmati par depend karega.

4. Aapke Sawal, Hamare Jawab (FAQs)

Kya company mujhe zabardasti 12 ghante kaam kara sakti hai?

Nahi. Naye codes flexibility dete hain, lekin zabardasti nahi. Overtime ke niyam ab pehle se zyada sakht hain aur agar aap zyada ghante kaam karte hain, toh company ko double rate par payment karni hogi.

"Retrenchment" kya hai aur kya meri naukri khatre mein hai?

Retrenchment ka matlab hai company se nikalna. Naye rules mein 300 tak employees wali companies ke liye process thoda asaan hua hai, lekin workers ke liye **Reskilling Fund** ka provision bhi laya gaya hai taki naukri jane par unhe nayi skill sikhne mein madad mile.

Maternity Benefit mein kya badlav hai?

Mahilaon ke liye naye codes kafi supportive hain. Ab woh har sector mein (night shifts samet) kaam kar sakti hain, basharte unki safety ka poora dhyan rakha jaye. Maternity benefits ko pehle se zyada streamline kiya gaya hai.
