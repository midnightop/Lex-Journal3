Social Account Hacked? The Legal Blueprint to Recovery in India

Expert Guidance by Anusmita Das

Modern Cyber Attacks

  • AI-Phishing: Scammers use AI-generated voices or videos to trick you into sharing OTPs.
  • SGI Fraud: Fake "Copyright Infringement" emails containing hidden malware links.
  • Session Hijacking: Stealing your browser "Cookies" to bypass Two Factor Authentication (2FA).

The Critical Timeline: When to Panic?

0-15 Minutes: The Golden Window

Check your email for a "Security Alert." Use the "Revert Change" link immediately. This is the fastest way to kick the hacker out.

1 Hour: Digital Documentation

Screenshot all emails about password changes and the hacker's new profile. These are your primary legal evidence.

3 Hours: The New Legal Rule

Under the 2026 IT Rules, platforms must respond to "Identity Theft" within 3 hours if reported through official Indian grievance channels.

Can I send a Legal Notice?

Absolutely. If Instagram, YouTube, or Meta fails to respond to your support ticket within 24 hours, you can serve a digital legal notice to their Grievance Officer in India.

Under Section 355 of BNSS, digital notices sent via Email or WhatsApp are fully valid in court.

Offline Strategy: Where to Contact?

If online support is failing, you must take these offline steps immediately:

  • Nearest Cyber Cell: Most cities now have dedicated Cyber Police Stations. Visit them to file a formal FIR.
  • National Helpline (1930): Dial this 24/7 helpline for instant reporting and technical blocking assistance.
  • Legal Counsel: Consult a cyber lawyer to draft a formal demand for "Account Restoration" under the IT Act.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many days will it take to get my account back?
If reported immediately, recovery usually takes 3-7 days. However, serving a legal notice to the platform can often speed this up to 48 hours.
Can the hacker be jailed?
Yes. Under the BNS (Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita), identity theft and cyber fraud carry a prison sentence of 3 to 7 years along with heavy financial penalties.
Is it mandatory to inform the police offline?
It is highly recommended, especially if the hacker is demanding money (Extortion) or posting illegal content. A police report provides you with "Legal Immunity" for any misuse of the account.
