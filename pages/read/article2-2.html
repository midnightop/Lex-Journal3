Section 103 of BNS: Is the new Murder Law different from IPC 302?

Analdeep Samanta

South Calcutta Law College

Ab tak humne filmon aur courts mein sirf ek hi dhara suni thi—IPC ki Dhara 302. Lekin 2026 mein, jab naya Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS) puri tarah laagu ho chuka hai, murder ki ye purani pehchan badal kar ab Section 103 BNS ho gayi hai.

1. Section 103(1): The Basic Definition

Simple shabdon mein kahein toh, agar koi kisi ka murder karta hai, toh use Section 103(1) ke tehat saza di jayegi. Yahan murder ki definition wahi hai jo pehle IPC mein thi, lekin ab iska serial number badal gaya hai.

Saza: Death Penalty (Maut ki saza) ya Life Imprisonment (Umar qaid) aur saath mein bhari fine.

2. Mob Lynching: Section 103(2) ka Naya Badlav

Ye is section ka sabse bada aur zaroori update hai. Pehle Mob Lynching (jab 5 ya usse zyada log milkar kisi ki jaan le lete hain) ke liye koi alag dhara nahi thi. Lekin ab BNS mein **Section 103(2)** ko khaas isi ke liye banaya gaya hai.

  • Agar 5 ya zyada log milkar Race, Caste, Community, Sex ya Language ke naam par kisi ka murder karte hain...
  • Toh group ke har member ko **Maut ki saza** ya **Life Imprisonment** di ja sakti hai.
Feature IPC (Old) BNS (New)
Section Number Section 302 Section 103
Mob Lynching No specific sub-section Specific Section 103(2)
Minimum Punishment Life Imprisonment Life Imprisonment

3. Kya Murder ki definition badal gayi?

Nahi. Section 101 BNS murder ko waisa hi define karta hai jaise IPC 300 karta tha. Murder ke liye wahi char essentials zaroori hain: Intent (Irada), Knowledge (Jankari), aur Bodily Injury jo maut ke liye kafi ho.

Key Takeaway for Law Students

Ab exam mein ya court practice mein IPC 302 cite karna band karna hoga. Case law abhi bhi purane wale (jaise Bachan Singh vs State of Punjab) kaam aayenge, kyunki "Rarest of Rare" doctrine abhi bhi valid hai, bas dhara ka naam 103 ho gaya hai.

4. Conclusion: Modern Law for Modern India

Section 103 sirf ek number ka badlav nahi hai, balki Mob Lynching jaise crimes ko legally recognize karne ki ek koshish hai. 2026 mein jab hum digital evidence (BSA) ka use kar rahe hain, tab ye naye sections justice system ko fast banane mein help kar rahe hain.
