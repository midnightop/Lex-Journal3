Modern Justice

BNS

Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, 2023

"De-colonizing Indian Criminal Jurisprudence."

BNS replaces the IPC to focus on justice rather than punishment. It introduces community service and modernizes definitions of organized crime and terrorism.

  • Zero FIR Implementation
  • Gender Neutrality in Crimes
  • Digital Records as Evidence
  • Simplified Section Mapping
