Constitutional Law

Constitution

The Constitution of India, 1950 • 448 Articles

The supreme law of India. It establishes the framework for fundamental political principles, procedures, powers, and duties of government institutions and sets out fundamental rights, directive principles, and the duties of citizens.

Longest written constitution in the world with 448 articles in 25 parts and 12 schedules.

Criminal Law

IPC

Indian Penal Code, 1860 • 511 Sections

The main criminal code of India, defining offences and prescribing punishments. Covers everything from theft to murder, and white-collar crimes to offences against the State.

Drafted by Lord Macaulay. Now being substantially replaced by Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS) 2023.
